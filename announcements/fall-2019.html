Biblical Counseling Course (Fall 2019)

We are eager to dig into the riches of 2 Corinthians 1-7 this fall, as we see together:

How the Lord uses the challenges of life, both circumstantial and relational
  • to greatly deepen our walks with Him
  • to grow in His character in practice
  • and to be equipped to help a lifetime of others.

We will be learning from the Lord through this description from Paul of the fundamental principles of his ministry including how to seek reconciliation in relations strained by misunderstanding.

Join us and spread the word by inviting others who may benefit as well as we learn together through a combination of presentation and interactive participation, also developing helpful methods and techniques for feeding oneself effectively and fruitfully on God's word.
