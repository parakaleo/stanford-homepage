Daily All-Campus Prayer

Daily Prayer

Join with fellow believers in Jesus for all-campus daily prayer: A great way to start your day!

Held every weekday (M-F) from 9:00-9:30am in the Old Union CIRCLE (3rd Floor) Common Room.
Open to all Stanford graduate and undergraduate students from all fellowships.

Come regularly or occasionally as the Lord leads.

Opening with: Reading of a brief passage of scripture & prayers of response to the Lord from it
Followed by: Group prayer, as the Holy Spirit leads individuals to pray for the campus, etc. (no listing of concerns, etc. but simply prayer, learning of concerns as others pray)
Optional follow-up time (9:30-10:00am) for: Sharing individual concerns, praying in small group(s), helping one another to find guidance from scripture, etc.)

Hosted by Parakaleo Christian Ministries as a service to all believers on campus.
