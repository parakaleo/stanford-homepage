Drop-In Prayer

Come join us for group and individual prayer!

  • Pray with others for the Lord's life-giving work on campus.
  • Receive individual prayer & encouragement/wisdom from God's Word for any personal concern and/or resources and guidance for helping others.
We meet on Mondays & Wednesdays from 4:00-5:00pm and Tuesdays & Thursdays from 9:30-10:30am in Old Union Room 314 (3rd floor)

Come regularly or occasionally as the Lord leads.
Or drop in for part of the time for specific prayer needs!

Open to all Stanford graduate and undergraduate students from all fellowships.

Hosted by Parakaleo Christian Ministries as a service to all believers on campus.
